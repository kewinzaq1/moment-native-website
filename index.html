Honest answers

Small on purpose.

Why only one technique?

Because choosing a method can become another delay. Box breathing is simple enough to remember when your attention is already compromised.

Why only one minute?

Because the first win is interruption. If one minute cannot make contact, twenty minutes usually becomes another performance.

Will it track me?

Not by default. Moment can work without an account, without analytics, and without needing to know who you are.

What happens after the minute?

You leave. That is the point. Moment should send you back into your life, not hold you inside another app.
